The Lord of the Rings Trilogy

About the Movies

The Lord of the Rings is an epic fantasy trilogy directed by Peter Jackson, based on J.R.R. Tolkien's novels.

The Trilogy:

  • The Fellowship of the Ring (2001)
  • The Two Towers (2002)
  • The Return of the King (2003)

Key Details

Director: Peter Jackson

Based on: The Lord of the Rings by J.R.R. Tolkien

Major Awards: 17 Academy Awards in total
