Amadeus

About the Movie

Amadeus is a 1984 biographical drama film about the life of Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart, told through the eyes of his rival Antonio Salieri.

Key Details

  • Director: Milos Forman
  • Release Year: 1984
  • Academy Awards: 8 Oscars, including Best Picture
  • Starring: F. Murray Abraham, Tom Hulce

Featured Music

Confutatis from Mozart's Requiem
